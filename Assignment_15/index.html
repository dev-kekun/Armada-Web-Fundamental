Spongebob SquarePants Characters

SpongBob SquarePants

SpongeBob is a childish, joyful, hardworking, and sometimes clumsy and silly sea sponge who lives in a pineapple with his pet snail Gary in the underwater city of Bikini Bottom.

Gary

Gary was originally intended to be a minor joke character that would appear in the background of SpongeBob's bedroom, just like SpongeBob's pet scallop Shelley.

Patrick Star

He lives under a rock in the underwater city of Bikini Bottom. SpongeBob and Squidward are his two neighbors, and, when viewed on the front side, are to the right of his residence.
